## SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION

# The World's First Decentralized Land Registry

![Clean diagram showing the 3-step process]

### How It Works

**Step 1: Community Validation**
```
Owner registers land
├─ GPS coordinates (exact location)
├─ Photos of the land
└─ 3 neighbors confirm: "Yes, this is their land"
```

**Step 2: Blockchain Proof**
```
Immutable record created
├─ Timestamped (exact date/time)
├─ Cannot be modified by ANYONE
└─ Not even by us (the company)
```

**Step 3: Peer-to-Peer Transaction**
```
When selling:
├─ Buyer and seller sign digitally
├─ Funds held in escrow (blockchain)
├─ Community validates transfer
└─ Money released automatically
```

**Result:** 48 hours instead of 6 months. 5% cost instead of 15%.
